Mengjing Wu

PhD Candidate at Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute (AAII), University of Technology Sydney

AAII

I am a PhD candidate at the Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute (AAII), University of Technology Sydney, supervised Dr Junyu Xuan and Dist.Prof. Jie Lu. My research interests mainly include Probabilistic Machine Learning, Bayesian Deep Learning, and Functional Bayesian Inference.

Currently, I focus on the functional inference for Bayesian neural networks (BNNs), including functional variational inference, functional MCMC, and functional diffusion. I am also interested in the geometric properties of the posterior landscape in BNNs.
